Chicken biryani is a popular and flavorful rice dish that originated in the Indian subcontinent. It is made by cooking rice with marinated chicken, aromatic spices, and sometimes with additional ingredients such as onions, tomatoes, yogurt, and various herbs. Chicken biryani is known for its rich and complex flavors, often featuring spices such as cumin, coriander, turmeric, cardamom, cinnamon, and cloves. It can be made in various regional styles, each with its own unique blend of spices and cooking techniques.
